Instagram Following Audit

Review who is not following back through a read-only script that runs locally in your Instagram tab.

No password collection. No account actions. No results leave your browser.

Run a scan

  1. Open instagram.com on a computer and sign in.
  2. Press F12, or right-click the page and choose Inspect.
  3. In the panel that opens, click the Console tab.
  4. Click Copy scanner code below, paste it into the Console, and press Enter.
  5. When the audit window appears on Instagram, click Run Scan.

If your browser blocks pasting in the Console, follow the on-screen prompt to enable pasting, then paste the code again.
